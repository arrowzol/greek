        written with:

            above and below: βζξ

    long vowels : ᾱηῑωῡ

    long only vowels : ηω
    short only vowels: εο
    short and long vowels: αιυ

punctuation (modern):
    (,)     break a clause
    (.)     period drawn low, end a sentence
    (·)     like english (:) or (;), above the line dot, raised dot
    (;)     Question
    (')     marks a final vowel dropped because it's followed by a leading vowel of another word

things to know:
    Capital letters are used less frequently in Greek than in English;
        only for the beginning of speech, paragraphs and names
        (i.e. not for the start of sentences).

keyboard accents, breathing, etc:
    accents (keyboard: ";[]" imagined as "/~\" accents):
        ;       άέίόύήώ     acute (/)
        ]       ὰὲὶὸὺὴὼ     grave (\)
        [       ᾶ-ῖ-ῦῆῶ     circumflex (~)
    smooth breathing marks (keyboard: single-quote, looks like smooth mark):
        '       ἀἐἰὀὐἠὠ     smooth (s)
        /       ἄἔἴὄὔἤὤ     smooth + acute
        =       ἂἒἲὂὒἢὢ     smooth + grave
        -       ἆ-ἶ-ὖἦὦ     smooth + circumflex
    rough breathing marks (keyboard: double-quote, shift the smooth mark):
        "       ἁἑἱὁὑἡὡ     rough (r)
        ?       ἅἕἵὅὕἥὥ     rough + acute
        +       ἃἓἳὃὓἣὣ     rough + grave
        _       ἇ-ἷ-ὗἧὧ     rough + circumflex
    iota subscripts (keyboard: opt-i, for "iota"):
        o-i     ᾳ----ῃῳ     iota subscript
        o-i;    ᾴ----ῄῴ     iota + acute
        o-i]    ᾲ----ῂῲ     iota + grave
        o-i[    ᾷ----ῇῷ     iota + circumflex
    iota subscripts and smooth breathing marks
        o-i'    ᾀ----ᾐᾠ     iota + smooth
        o-i';   ᾄ----ᾔᾤ     iota + smooth + acute
        o-i'[   ᾆ----ᾖᾦ     iota + smooth + circumflex
        o-i']   ᾂ----ᾒᾢ     iota + smooth + grave
    iota subscripts and rough breathing marks
        o-i"    ᾁ----ῇῷ     iota + rough
        o-i";   ᾅ----ᾕᾥ     iota + rough + acute
        o-i"[   ᾇ----ᾗᾧ     iota + rough + circumflex
        o-i"]   ᾃ----ᾓᾣ     iota + rough + grave
    long and short vowels (keyboard: opt-l/shift-opt-l, l for "long"):
        o-l     ᾰ-ῐ-ῠ--     short vowels (S)
        s-o-l   ᾱ-ῑ-ῡ--     long vowels (L)
                ᾱ́-?-?--
    vowel stuff:
        :       --ϊ-ϋ--     umlaut - not a diphthong
        W       --ΐ-ΰ--     umlaut + acute

    punctuation (modern):
        ,   ,   comma (as in English)
        .   .   period (as in English)
        o-9 ·   raised dot - like English (: or ;)
        q   ;   question - like english (?)
        ᾿       missing vowel at the end of certain prepositions
